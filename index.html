üìÖ Sample 5-Day Itinerary

This is a suggested itinerary. Adjust based on your actual travel dates and preferences!
Day 1: Travel Day
Morning: Fly from Melbourne, FL to Calgary (likely 1-2 connections)
Afternoon: Land in Calgary, pick up rental car, drive to Canmore (1.5 hours)
Evening: Check into lodging, grocery shopping at Save-On-Foods or Safeway, settle in, early night to adjust to time zone (2 hour difference)
Dinner: Cook something simple or grab pizza at Rocky Mountain Flatbread Co.
Day 2: Lake Louise
Morning: Early start (8am), drive to Lake Louise (45 min), first chair at 9am
All Day: Explore Canada's largest ski resort - start with groomers to warm up, then hit the back bowls
Lunch: On mountain (Whitehorn Lodge or pack sandwiches)
Evening: Back to Canmore, cook dinner, hit Iron Goat Pub or relax
Notes: Lake Louise is HUGE - you could spend 3 days here and not see it all
Day 3: Sunshine Village
Morning: Drive to Sunshine Village (35 min), gondola up at 9am
All Day: Powder paradise - explore the alpine terrain and tree runs
Lunch: Chimney Corner Lounge (mid-mountain) or Eagle's Nest
Evening: Return to Canmore, brewery visit (Grizzly Paw or Canmore Brewing Co.)
Notes: Sunshine gets the most snow - if there's fresh powder, this is where you want to be
Day 4: Mount Norquay + Banff Town
Morning: Half day at Mount Norquay (20 min from Canmore) - smaller but steep and fun
Afternoon: Drive into Banff town for lunch and exploring (15 min from Norquay)
Activities: Walk around Banff Avenue, check out Cascade of Time Garden, photos at Bow Falls
Evening: Dinner in Banff (Block Kitchen or Melissa's Missteak) or back to Canmore
Optional: If legs are tired, skip morning skiing and do Banff + Johnston Canyon icewalk
Day 5: Flex Day / Travel Home
Option A (Morning flight): Early checkout, return car at Calgary, fly home
Option B (Afternoon/evening flight): Morning at Norquay or Lake Louise, then travel home
Option C (Extra day): Hit your favorite resort from earlier in the week or try the one you haven't done yet
Notes: Build in flexibility here based on flight times and how your legs are feeling!
Bonus Activities if You Have Time:
‚Ä¢ Johnston Canyon frozen waterfall hike (must-do if you have time!)
‚Ä¢ Banff Upper Hot Springs (perfect for sore muscles)
‚Ä¢ Lake Minnewanka for winter scenery
‚Ä¢ Canmore Nordic Centre for cross-country skiing
